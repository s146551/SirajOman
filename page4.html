Discover Oman's Breathtaking Landscapes

Jebel Shams - The "Mountain of Sun"

Oman's mountain ranges offer some of the most spectacular scenery in the Arabian Peninsula. The Al Hajar mountains run like a backbone through the country, creating dramatic canyons and traditional villages that seem suspended in time.

Must-Visit Mountain Destinations:

  • Jebel Shams: Oman's highest peak at 3,009 meters
  • Jebel Akhdar: The "Green Mountain" famous for rose gardens
  • Jabal Samhan: Home to the endangered Arabian leopard
  • Wadi Ghul: Known as the "Grand Canyon of Arabia"

The Golden Deserts

Oman's deserts are not just vast empty spaces, but living landscapes with unique ecosystems and rich Bedouin heritage. The dunes tell stories of ancient trade routes and nomadic traditions.

Desert Experiences Featured in Siraj Oman:

  1. Sunset dune bashing in Wahiba Sands
  2. Traditional Bedouin camping under the stars
  3. Camel trekking across rolling dunes
  4. Stargazing in the clear desert skies
  5. Visiting desert oasis settlements
