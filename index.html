Welcome to Siraj Oman

Our Project: Siraj Oman

Our project is called "Siraj Oman," which means "Lantern of Oman." It will be a simple and easy-to-use website. The main feature is an interactive map of Oman that helps tourists and locals find interesting places to visit.

Discover Amazing Places in Oman

Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque

One of the most beautiful mosques in the world, located in Muscat. Features stunning Islamic architecture and the world's second-largest hand-woven carpet.

Muscat

Wahiba Sands

Vast desert with towering dunes, perfect for camel riding, dune bashing, and experiencing traditional Bedouin culture.

Al Sharqiyah

Nizwa Fort

Historical fortress from the 17th century with massive circular tower, offering panoramic views of the city and date palm plantations.

Nizwa

Wadi Shab

Beautiful canyon with emerald pools, waterfalls, and caves. Requires a short hike and swim to reach the hidden waterfall.

Al Sharqiyah
