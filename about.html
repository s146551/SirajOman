About Siraj Oman

Our Mission

Siraj Oman, which means "Lantern of Oman," is dedicated to illuminating the beauty and richness of Oman for both tourists and locals. Our mission is to create the most comprehensive and user-friendly platform for discovering the hidden gems and popular attractions across this beautiful country.

What We Do

We provide an interactive map-based platform that helps you:

  • Discover amazing places across all regions of Oman
  • Find activities that match your interests
  • Get accurate information about opening hours and locations
  • Read reviews and recommendations from other travelers

Why Choose Siraj Oman?

Comprehensive Database

We cover all governorates and wilayas of Oman, from Muscat to Dhofar.

User-Friendly

Simple, intuitive interface designed for everyone to use easily.

Always Updated

Regular updates with new locations and current information.

Free to Use

Completely free service for all users - locals and tourists alike.

Our Story

Siraj Oman was born from a simple observation: while Oman is filled with incredible places to visit, finding comprehensive and organized information can be challenging. We wanted to create a central platform that makes exploration easy and enjoyable.

Our team consists of Omani students who are passionate about sharing the beauty of our country with the world. We believe that every corner of Oman has something special to offer, and we're committed to helping you discover it.
